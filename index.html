Janet Currie

Chicago Cityscape Photographer

About

Janet is a passionate Chicago-based photographer known for her evocative black and white cityscapes. Her work captures the energy, architecture, and hidden beauty of Chicago, from its iconic skyline to its vibrant neighborhoods. Janet’s love for photography began in her teens, inspired by the city’s ever-changing light and dramatic weather. She believes every street and building tells a story, and she strives to share those stories through her lens.

When she’s not behind the camera, Janet enjoys exploring new coffee shops, volunteering at local art programs, and mentoring young photographers in the community.

Career

With over a decade of professional experience, Janet’s photography has been featured in major galleries, magazines, and public installations throughout Chicago. She has collaborated with local artists, contributed to city tourism campaigns, and held solo exhibitions at venues such as the Chicago Art Institute and Navy Pier Gallery.

  • 2023: Solo exhibition at Millennium Park Art Pavilion
  • 2024: Featured in “Chicago in Focus” magazine
  • 2025: Group show at Navy Pier Gallery
  • 2026: Lead photographer for Chicago’s “Open Streets” project

Janet is also a frequent guest speaker at photography workshops and has received several awards for her unique perspective on urban life.
